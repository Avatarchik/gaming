PowerXSTtream®

  • All PowerXStream wins pay left-to-right and right-to-left, on adjacent reels starting with the leftmost or rightmost reel.
  • The award is multiplied by the number of symbols that occur per reel in the winning combination.
  • Only highest award is paid per PowerXStream combination.

XStatic Wilds™

  • The SUN symbol substitutes for all symbols except BONUS.
  • The SUN symbol appears on reel 3 only.
  • During the free spins, a SUN symbol on the middle reel will remain in that position for the remainder of the free spins.

Streaming Stacks®

  • At the start of each spin, a symbol is randomly chosen to be stacked on reels 1, 2, 4 and 5.
  • All symbols may not be available for replacement.

Additional Game Rules and Instructions

  • Choose SPIN to spin or repeat the last bet.
  • Select Multiplier Bet Level (1x to 5x).
  • Bonus wins are added to wins displayed in the WON meter.
  • Higher credit denominations: 1K=1000, 1M=1 000 000, 1B=1 000 000 000.
  • The Won field displays winnings in credits.
  • Malfunction Voids All Pays and Play.
  • The WILD symbol substitutes for all symbols except BONUS and SUN.
  • The WILD symbol appears on reels 2 and 4 in the free spins bonus only.

Free Spins Bonus

  • Each winning combination of BONUS symbol on reels 2, 3 and 4 award Free Spins.
  • Multiple combinations can occur in a single game as follows:
    • 1 winning combination awards 10 free spins.
    2 winning combinations awards 15 free spins.
    3 winning combinations awards 20 free spins.
  • Additional free spins cannot be won during the Free Spins play.
  • Different Reels are used during Free Spins play.
  • Jackpot cannot be won during Free Spins play.

Auto Spin:

  • In jurisdictions where this feature is available, you can have your bet repeated for a selectable number of spins.
  • In settings, choose Auto Spin "ON" and select the desired number of autospins.
  • Select the STOP button to cancel Autospin.
  • Autospin must be reactivated after a bonus.
  • Autospin is deactivated after a bonus or feature.
