Game Features
3 Free Spin icons on any reels awards 10 free games. Additional 10 free games awarded if retriggered within a free spin.
Any star icon appearing on any reel is added to the "Stars" count. Stars collected persist across game sessions. Stars do not pay and are included to the use of the persistent accumulator feature.
